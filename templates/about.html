About Ningali Asta

Ningali Asta is a web-based application designed to bridge the communication gap between the hearing-impaired community and the general public.

By leveraging Machine Learning and Computer Vision technologies (specifically MediaPipe and TensorFlow), we translate SIBI (Sistem Isyarat Bahasa Indonesia) hand gestures into alphabets in real-time.

Our mission is to make communication inclusive and accessible for everyone.
